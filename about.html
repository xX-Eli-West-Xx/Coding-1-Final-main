About Us!

We are a large group of trucks that go around the US to certain locations to clean your pets. We know that having a pet can be expensive at times, so we make sure that cleaning shouldn't be one of those big expenses.


We take pride in how our pets look. We think you should too! Come any time! No appointment necessary!

Our pets get stressed as well. Take them out. Treat them to a spa day. They will feel rejuvinated, and ready for the next day! We all just need a good relax.

Christmas is coming up soon! Why not get something for your pet? Pets in the past have been thrilled to come to our spa trucks!
